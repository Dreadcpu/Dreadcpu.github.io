The Game

Master the Momentum. In VOLTWAVE, your survival is determined by one simple rule: Keep Moving. You are a vector sphere in a dark geometric void.

Use precision flicking to slice through waves of distinct, neon-red geometric demons. Hit a target, regain your turn, and chain your flicks into a devastating combo of neon kinetic energy.

10 Waves. 10 Minutes.

The demo features the first 10 waves of the Void. Each wave introduces new tactical threats that require split-second decision making and perfect execution.

The Demons

The void is populated by adaptive geometric threats. Each one requires a unique strategy to dismantle.

The Chaser

Standard fodder. Moves slowly but directly toward you. Do not let them swarm.

The Splitter

On impact, it fractures into three smaller, high-speed triangles. Plan your chain-flick before you strike.

The Repulsor

Protected by a kinetic force field. It pushes you away—only a high-velocity strike can pierce its core.

About Dreadcpu

Dreadcpu is a solo-developer project focused on restoring the mechanical purity, satisfaction, and challenge of golden-age arcade gameplay.

Built with Godot 4, VOLTWAVE is the result of a "fail-fast" philosophy: focusing on tight game-feel and innovative physics above all else. Every line of code and every vector outline is crafted to deliver that "one more try" addiction.
